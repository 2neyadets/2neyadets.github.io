About this website

I made this page when I finished learning HTML5 and CSS3. Here you will not find .js files. In my opinion, this page turned out simple and pleasant. I specifically did not use Bootstrap 4 when creating this page in order to improve the skills of positioning (layout). In the page's code, you can find the use of tools like "display: grid" or "display: flex". I wanted to make the page extremely responsive and scalable. You can check me out by changing the size of your browser window. Or if you watch it from your mobile, flip it.
